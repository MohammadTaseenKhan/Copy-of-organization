Activities Planned for November 2014

IR 1: Improved Governance of Natural Resources and Biodiversity

Next month activity plan of November, 2014

  1. Facilitate CMC meeting
  2. Facilitate CM Council Meeting
  3. Facilitate PF (GB) Meeting
  4. Facilitate PF(EB) Meeting
  5. Facilitate CPG meeting
  6. Facilitate VCF(EB) meeting
  7. Facilitate VCF (GB)meeting
  8. Facilitate UP SC meeting
  9. Facilitate NS Refreshers Training
  10. CMO  Assessment
  11. Gender score card orientation
  12. Gender score card survey in the field
  13. Nishorgo Network meeting
  14. Different types of workshop
  15. UP Standing Committee meeting

IR2: Strengthened Organizational Capacity of NRM Institutions. 

  1. 3 trainings on Natural resources and environmental laws/acts/policy for Upazila, UP staffs and CMO members
  2. 1 Training for CMO members and organization management and leadership development
  3. 10 CCAM and CRNRM training at VCF level
  4. 1 training for nursery establishment
  5. Skill development Training on climate resilient livelihoods specific trades for the farmers based on the farming calendar and link with the buyers- 590  (HNP-100, MNKP-40, FWS-100, whykong-125, Teknaf-150, Shilkhali-75)
  6. 4 Grants and Financial management training
  7. 2 Fund raising plan and proposal writing trainings
  8. 1 Gender score card orientation training
  9. 3 trainings on Gender Mainstreaming in NRM
  10. 1 Communication training for CMO, RMO, VCF, PF for communication with GoB and Media
  11. 1 IPT training workshop
  12. 1 PCVA training for CREL staffs
  13. 1 Orientation workshop for journalists
  14. 1 Regional Consultation workshop to assess options for at source collection
  15. 1 stakeholder engagement workshop at regional level on CMO lessons and best practices focused on CMC structure performances
  16. 1 workshop on ECA rules sharing with stakeholders for incorporating co-management and VCG members
  17. 5 planning sessions for ADP development

IR 3: Strengthen Implementation of climate resilient NRM

  • Preparatory work for 3 Management plans development (MKNP, Inani, TWS)
  • Conduction of PCVA in new sites Sonadia, St. Martin and Inani sites
  • Nurserer and nursery site selection, IEE documents preparation
  • Site selection & beneficiary identification for Bamboo clump management
  • Nursery training module development
  • Development of 12 Nursery in 6 sites
  • Development of 5 turtle hatcheries
  • Site selection for bird and bat colonies
  • Turtle egg collection and hatching and preparatory work related to safe release
  • Control of feral dog
  • Identification of VCFs & VCG location in new sites  (Inani, St. Martin, and of new sites
  • Fish catch monitoring
  • Follow up 6 Garjan seed sowing sites
  • Mulching and grazing control of plantation sites
  • Participatory Forest Resident Bird survey finding sharing.
  • Continue CrelLink data entry progress monitoring.
  • Beat level report preparation based on PCVA

IR4: Improved Livelihoods that are Environmentally Sustainable and Resilient to Climate Change

  • Selection of 300 LH beneficiaries for Doll making training
  • Organize 150 selected beneficiaries for participating employment training
  • Provide Market Development Capacity of the LSPs to strengthen their market link with their farmers -50 LSP
  • Development of LSP business plan training-50 (HNP-10, MNKP-8, FWS-8, whykong-7, Teknaf-10, Shilkhali-7)
  • Skill development Training on climate resilient livelihoods specific trades for the farmers based on the farming calendar and link with the buyers- 590  (HNP-100, MNKP-40, FWS-100, whykong-125, Teknaf-150, Shilkhali-75)
  • Exposure visit- 1 ( individual reflection of strawberry cultivation –HNP-5, MNKP-3, FWS-7, whykong-3, Teknaf-7, Shilkhali-1)
  • Model village establishment- 1 (Medhakachpia)
  • Demonstration technology for better production- 1 (Fasiakahali)
  • Demonstration of commercial homestead gardening technology-35 (whykong-25, Fasiakhali-10)

Cross-Cutting Components 

Gender

  • 1 NS orientation will be held covering 45 participants

Communications

  • IPT Group Meeting.
  • Youth club messages prepare.
  • Communication with GO, NGO and other stakeholders Message board setup on nature conservation.
  • News publication
  • Coordination with Dhaka communication team for valuable work of communication perspective fulfill of last quarter of Year 2 Plan.
  • Masjid Campaign message prepare by the Imam and Moulanas instruct by Dhaka CREL team
  • Activities of Radio Journalism going on.
  • New program of Radio Journalism Month of October.
  • School Awareness program
  • Ongoing Communication with Religious Leaders (Masjid Campaign).
  • Communication with IPT group and practice for new IPT Script and training.
  • Two Success Story Prepare.
  • Regular Communication with FD, DoE, DoF, GO, NGO person and organizations.

Grants

  • Training/TOT on Grants implementation, monitoring & reporting and compliances (for regional and site based staff)- 1 TOT
  • Post-award training on Financial Management, record keeping, managing projects, reporting and auditing (Round 1 for 6 CMCs)- 06 training.
  • Facilitation for implementation of grants projects activities (Capacity building towards sustainability, biodiversity conservation, biophysical improvement, climate change adaptation, PA/Tourism infrastructure, etc.)-06 Project.
  • Grants monitoring, and reporting; documentation-06 Project.
  • Circular to local paper for CMC Accounts and admin Assistant recruitment.
  • Conduct interview for CMC Accounts cum Admin Assistant recruitment. FD, RC, Grants Officer & CMC office bearer will present
  • Assist to CMC for deliverable report preparation & Contact papers preparation with CMC AAO.
  • Assist to CMC for conduct six project inception orientation by six CMC.

Monitoring and Evaluation 

  • Conduct final Data Quality Assessments (DQA) at all site offices (Nov, 3-4)
  • Conduct CMO assessment at 7 CMCs (Nov, 5-12)
  • CrelLink data entry facilitation (Regular)
  • Foreign trip to Vietnam to attend ‘Regional Knowledge Sharing on Participatory Forest Monitoring’ and REDD+ discussion (Nov, 13-21)
  • Waterbird census at Nijhum Dwip (Nov, 24-30)
  • Y2 Participatory Forest Bird Monitoring report sharing at 5 CMCs (TBD)
